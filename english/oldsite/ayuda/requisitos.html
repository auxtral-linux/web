Hardware requirements

 

 

While Auxtral 2 does not have strict minimum system requirements, I would recommend at least the following specs to have a good experience:

  • Intel 64-bit 1.6 Ghz processor (2.5 Ghz recommended)
  • 2 GB of RAM (4 Gb recommended)
  • A disk partition with 20 Gb of free space (40 Gb recommended)
  • Internet access
  • 1280 × 720 px screen resolution (1920x1080 recommended)
